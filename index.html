About

BEC, Branded Educational Consulting, was founded by Becca Gibson, a seasoned college professor of psychology. Her experience in the world of higher education taught her a very important lesson. Creating an environment of stability and excellence affords students the opportunity to build trust in their professors, fellow students and their overall educational surroundings. This trust led to deeper relationships and greater understanding, which enabled students to excel in the various roles they entered into upon graduating.

Becca brings these skills to local schools and universities in order to in order to empower faculty and staff to create their own environments for growth. These environments help teach students how to go out and change the world.
